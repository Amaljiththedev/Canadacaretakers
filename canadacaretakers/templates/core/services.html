What We Do

At Canada's Caretakers, we offer a range of professional services designed to keep your property in top condition throughout the year. From lawn care to snow removal, our team is here to meet all your property maintenance needs.

Lawn Care

Our lawn care services ensure that your lawn remains lush and healthy. We handle everything from regular mowing to seasonal fertilization and weed control.

Snow Removal

Stay safe and accessible during winter with our efficient snow removal services. We clear driveways, walkways, and other areas to prevent accidents and keep you moving.

Moving Services

Our moving services make relocation hassle-free. From packing to transportation and unpacking, we handle all aspects of your move with care and professionalism.

Driveway Sealing

Protect your driveway from the elements with our sealing services. We use high-quality sealants to extend the lifespan of your driveway and enhance its appearance.

Seasonal Cleanups

We offer comprehensive seasonal cleanups to keep your property in top shape. From autumn leaf removal to springtime garden prep, we handle it all.

Property Maintenance

Our property maintenance services ensure that every aspect of your property is well-maintained. We address everything from minor repairs to major renovations.
